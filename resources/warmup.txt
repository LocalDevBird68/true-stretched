WRIST WARMUP
-interlace fingers and hands, spin for 15 seconds

-curve fingers as far up hand as possible, apply pressure to fingers with other palm and use fingertips to bend the hand
    -repeat on both hands
    -stretches up the palm of the hand with curved fingertips
    
-then keep fingers closed, point hand down as far as possible, and push it back further with other hand
    -make sure arm is straight
    -might need to ground elbow to table so you can apply more pressure
    -stretch up forearm

RANGE WARMUP

- use iso
- if you start noticing constant tension, stop and fix it
- dont go too fast, do it as fast as you can accurately
    - in other words dont break ur wrist trying to flick frantically fast, take ur time ur warming up

PHANTOM

-practice spray control & spray transfer
    -vary time/distance and adjust the mouse movement as needed
    -try to make the spread as tight as possible, more dense
    -headshot a bot, start spraying and transfer to 3 other bots, rinse n repeat

-do some deadzoning n counter strafing while shooting
    -track one point, dz and shoot or cs and shoot
    -lrll/rlrr as well dont forget

-turn on medium bots, follow stop timing drill
    -strafe, adjust to the head, then counterstrafe and shoot, don’t flick to middle
        -once with deadzoning once with cs
    -in other words, move while aiming

-track a bots head for 3 seconds while strafing, then flick quickly to another bot, rinse and repeat

-strafe and shoot a little bit
    -deadzone deadzone 2 bullet burst w/ cs, 2 bullet burst w/ cs
    - lrll/rlrr

-pop shield
    -counter strafe on bots, deadzone on shield balls
    -vice versa
