PHANTOM

-practice spray control & spray transfer
    -vary time/distance and adjust the mouse movement as needed
    -try to make the spread as tight as possible, more dense
    -headshot a bot, start spraying and transfer to 3 other bots, rinse n repeat

-do some deadzoning n counter strafing while shooting
    -only one step = no need for cs

-turn on elim 50, follow stop timing drill
    -strafe, adjust to the head, then counterstrafe and shoot, don’t flick to middle
        -once with deadzoning once with cs
    -in other words, move while aiming

-track a bots head for 3 seconds while strafing, then flick quickly to another bot, rinse and repeat

-strafe and shoot a little bit
    -deadzone deadzone 2 bullet burst w/ cs, 2 bullet burst w/ cs
    - lrll/rlrr

-pop shield
    -counter strafe on bots, deadzone on shield balls
    -vice versa
